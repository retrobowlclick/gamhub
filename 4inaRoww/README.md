grrd's 4 in a Row
=================

grrd's 4 in a Row is a HTML5 connect-4 game for one or two players. 
Who is the first to put 4 stones horizontally, vertically or diagonally in a line? 
grrd's 4 in a Row is free, no ads, no in-app purchases.
